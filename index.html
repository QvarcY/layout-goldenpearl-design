Mangata & Gallo

Mangata and Gallo is a jewelery store that specializes in special occasions like engagements, weddings and anniversaries. The jewelry company primarily operates online and has a small storefront in Austin, Texas with an atelier attached for browsing. Mangata & Gallo's selection of jewelry is known for its high-quality and classic look. The owner, an Austin local, is well known for her jewelry designs.

Jewelery Mas Fits

Necklaces, earrings, and rings may all be small pieces of jewelry, but that doesn’t mean the story behind each piece is small. In fact, jewelry allows us to proudly remember our biggest milestones and triumphs. Everyone’s personal journey is completely different and even the same pieces of jewelry can convey separate meanings spending on the wearer. It’s time that we take a step back and appreciate what jewelry means to each individual person, even if we don’t know the full story.
